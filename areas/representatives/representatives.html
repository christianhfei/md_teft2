My Representatives

What is a representative?

A representative is a person who is allowed access to your MyCare account to help you manage your care. This person is usually a trusted friend or family member. Select an option from below to continue.

View My Representatives

This section will let you:

View a list of representatives from your profile in LTSS, created by your supports planner with your help.

Invite a Representative

This section will let you:

Select a representative and invite them to manage your care using the MyLTSS system.
