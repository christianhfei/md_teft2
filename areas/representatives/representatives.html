My Representatives

What is a representative?

A representative is a person who you approved with your supports planner to help manage your health information and services. You are not required to have a representative and not all participants have a representative. Contact your supports planner if you have questions about your representative. By allowing your representative to access the MyLTSS system, they will have all of the same abilities as you to review your service information.
